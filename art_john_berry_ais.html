John has always been interested in the comic arts, as illustrated by his collection of Daria artwork and fanfic (most of which is comedic in nature). It's therefore no surprise that he'd create a comic strip based on Daria... "Adventures in Shallowness," starring everyone's favorite fashion flakes, the Fashion Club!
