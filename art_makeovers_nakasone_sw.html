Shaun Nakasone's
Star Wars Episode I Makeovers

Shaun Nakasone (art_geckohawaii@hotmail.com) is a huge Star Wars fan, so much so that he was inspired to create these makeovers based on Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace. Since there were so many of them, they have been collected onto a single page rather than splitting them onto the individual character makeover pages.
