Nicole Berry is the sister of John Berry, well known for his humorous fanfic and artwork. According to John, who focuses his artistic talents on cartoons, his sister's artwork leans more towards realism. These wonderful sketches of our favorite Lawndale residents bear that out, and have earned a well-deserved page of their own in the Outpost Daria art gallery.
