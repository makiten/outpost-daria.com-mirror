Characters


Each character page includes a biography with the most complete information about the character to date (age, siblings, parents, vocation, voice talent, and the episode in which they first appeared), as well as any "alter-ego" images that exist. (Alter-egos are the pictures of the characters in various costumes, which are shown during the end credits of the episodes.)

Note: Some character images were scanned from The Daria Diaries and The Daria Database and captured from various Daria episodes. Images of Daria, Helen, Jake, Quinn, Jane, Trent, Andrea, Brittany, Jodie, Kevin, Mack, Upchuck, Sandi, Stacy, Tiffany, Joey, Jeffy, Jamie, and Tom were created specifically for this site by Daria fan artist Liliane Grenier (thanks, Liliane!).
