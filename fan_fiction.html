Fan Fiction


The Daria universe as shown on the TV screen was a rich one, but there are plenty of stories that (for many reasons) simply couldn't be told there. Enter fan fiction ("fanfic"), where your favorite Lawndale residents are placed in all kinds of new situations by authors who love the characters as much as you do. Some stories are ordinary, others are downright bizzare, but you can bet that they're always an adventure!

Note: Stories sorted by author are sorted alphabetically by the first letter of the author's name, in order to accomodate handles and abbreviations that don't follow the first name/last name pattern. Thus, "Joe Smith" would be listed under "J" instead of "S."
