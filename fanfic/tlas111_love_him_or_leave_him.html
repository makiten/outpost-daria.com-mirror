Daria: No, but…

AP: Can I call a cease-fire?

Lynn: For now.

AP: (lowers shield) Cool. This thing is heavy. (beat; to Daria, shy) Hey.

Daria: (shy) Hey.

(Lynn and Jane share "Here we go again" looks.)
