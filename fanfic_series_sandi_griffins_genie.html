Prologue

After school on a Wednesday afternoon in the February of her sophomore year, Sandi Griffin makes a seemingly innocuous discovery.
#1: The Beginning

After she releases the genie from the emerald, Sandi decides to take the emerald to school. Cue strange events at recess, disrupted classes and intrigued classmates.
#2: Friday Adventures

Quinn, Stacy and Tiffany plan, Jane tells Trent about the previous day, Sam Griffin discovers Sandi's secret, Andrea gets in trouble with Ms. Li, and one of Jane's former track team members investigates the new floor on the Science Block.
#3: School Changes

When the emerald gets out of Sandi's hands, chaos occurs at Lawndale High.
