TitleDescription
Part IQuinn's ex-boyfriend puts Lawndale on the map when he shoots up Lawndale High. Everyone wants to know how much Daria and Quinn knew about it beforehand, and are they victims or accomplices? The Lawndale PD believe the latter, and they have evidence.
Part IIAs the Morgendorffers deal with the aftermath of the shooting, Daria and Quinn become pawns in a political and media circus, and find out that the threat Matthew posed isn't entirely over.
Part IIIIn the final chapter of the series, as the political and media circus continues, the Morgendorffers come to realize that there are darker forces at work than mere police bungling and violent drug dealers.
