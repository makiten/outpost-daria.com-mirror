Part 1: We All Fall Down

Daria and Quinn make a bet to switch places for a week. They both know they're letting themselves in for a rough week, but no one could foresee all the consequences of this one little switch.
Part 2: All the King's Horses

Two months have passed since the fallout from Daria and Quinn's fateful bet engulfed Lawndale High. The sisters are now ready to deal with that fallout, but how far will they go, and who will be pulled into their schemes?
