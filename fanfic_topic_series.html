Fan Fiction

Sometimes an adventure just can't be bound to a single story. Here's where you'll find tales ranging from simple multi-part stories to epic series that would take days to read (but would be worth every minute!).

Authors: If you no longer wish for any of your stories to be posted, please send e-mail to fanfic@lawndale.net and I will remove them at once.

And, as always, please do not spam the authors on these pages! Their e-mail addresses are being provided as a means for you to send your comments about their fan fiction; please do not abuse the privilege!

Note: (*) indicates that the link to the story is to another website. Such stories will be opened in a new browser window (or tab).
