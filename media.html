In the
Media


Daria has been featured often in the media ever since (and, in some cases, even before) it premiered in March of 1997. Appearances have ranged from serious reviews of the show by newspapers such as The New York Times and The Washington Post, to lighter pieces in Seventeen and People magazines, and even a parody in Cracked magazine.

Note that most of these references were obtained from the Planet Daria and alt.lawndale.com web sites. If you spot any references that you think would make a good addition to this list, send e-mail to webmaster@lawndale.net and I'll do my best to add them.
