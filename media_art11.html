In the Media

MTV Video Music Awards
September 4, 1997
Daria and Jane were on the MTV Video Music Awards on September 4. Right before one of the commercial breaks, a clip of Daria and Jane sitting on a bed watching TV was shown. Here is the transcript:

Daria - Wake up. It's almost over.

Jane - Did I miss anything?

Daria - Yeah. It was great.

Jane (surprised) - Daria?

Daria (mumbles) - Damn contract.
