Episode #501
Fizz Ed
  • Ms. Li schedules the school meeting for Super Bowl Sunday. However, the Lawndale Lions are playing a game later in the episode. High school football is generally in the fall, and is over well before Super Bowl Sunday.
  • When Ms. Li is taken away in the ambulance, the ambulance changes from a panel van to a cutaway van.

Episode #502
Sappy Anniversary
  • In the flashback scene, Jake is shown holding the book in one particular way. In the close-up of Helen, the edge of the book shows that it has changed positions without Jake doing anything.
  • After Jake freaks out during his presentation, Noah Barkman says, "Wasn't it great, everyone?" and leads a round of applause. Here, the neck piece of his turtle neck is missing. Then, when he starts to say, "You really helped us get into the mindset of the average Joe," it mysteriously returns.

Episode #503
Fat Like Me
  • Before the commercial break, Sandi's cast is on her left leg. After the break, the cast is on her right leg.
  • When Stacy says "Because I can't take it anymore!" in the bathroom, the hairbrush she was holding disappears when she puts her hands down.
