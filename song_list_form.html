Song List
Submission Form

The following form can be used to submit to Outpost Daria any information about the music that appears in Daria episodes. Use this form to correct information about current song list entries, or to add new songs to the lists. The information will be submitted as an e-mail message.

Note: This form is to be used ONLY for submitting entries to the song lists! Questions and other forms of correspondence should be addressed to webmaster@lawndale.net, as they will NOT be answered from this form!
