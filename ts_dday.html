Transcript of
MTV's "Daria Day" 1998

MTV held a "Daria Day" marathon on the date of the premiere of the second season (February 16, 1998), showing most of the episodes of the first season to whet the fans' appetites. The following is a transcript of the bridging sequences aired during the marathon, which featured Daria and Jane chatting it up about the episodes and answering questions submitted by viewers via MTV Online. Aside from being generally entertaining, the chatter answered a couple of interesting questions about the characters (such as their ages).


Throughout the marathon, Daria and Jane were shown standing in front of a window overlooking New York's Times Square, as seen above. The animation was limited (but not so much as the later Sarcastathon 3000), consisting mainly of lip-sync and facial expressions. The Times Square footage was live action and added movement to an otherwise static setting. Also, each segment was introduced with a rapid montage of iconic Daria images. These included some familiar alter-egos, as well as live-action close ups of items such as Daria's glasses and boots, and Quinn's smiley face T-shirt (which confirmed that she is as well-endowed in real life as in her animated form).
