What's New

Outpost Daria is updated on an as-needed basis. This is due mainly to two reasons: my personal life (and the relationships therein), which always comes first, and the low number of submissions to the site (due mainly to Daria ending its initial run on MTV and its scarcity in second-run syndication). Rest assured, however, that updates will continue to be made, at least for the forseeable future. You ain't rid of me yet! <grin>
