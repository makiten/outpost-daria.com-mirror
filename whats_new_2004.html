May 2004
May 25 I'm lifting the "no partial stories" requirement for fanfic submissions. I figured that since there were very few stories being submitted these days, it wasn't really fair to make authors of multi-part stories wait until every chapter was finished before allowing them to submit it. So, authors, fire away!

By the same token, I'm going to start updating more frequently, even if there are only one or two new items in the update. This should get stuff out to you more quickly, so you won't have to wait weeks (or months) between updates.

February 2004
Feb 29 Yeah, I know... I haven't updated in months, this update is pretty thin in the skin, where the hell have I been, etc. Things like that happen when you get very few submissions because people think you aren't updating, which causes updates to be fewer, which causes even more people to think you aren't updating, and soon it becomes one of those viscious things! ("Where will it end, Daria? Where will it end?") Unfortunately, that just may end up being the pattern for a while, so you should probably get used to it. I'm still not going anywhere, though, so DON'T PANIC!!!
