July 2009
This may be the last update for a while, as I am scheduled to undergo bariatric surgery on July 29th. I'll be laid up, then concentrating on getting back on normal food, throughout August and much of September. But please, don't hesitate to send any fanfic, artwork, or other submissions; I'll store 'em and get to 'em when I can.

(For those who are curious, the procedure I'm having is a new one called a "sleeve gastrectomy." It basically separates and seals off 85% of my stomach, leaving a small, banana-shaped pouch behind, with the intent being to restrict the amount of food I can eat. Combined with a healthy diet and exercise, I expect to lose quite a bit of weight, with my goal being to drop at least 200 pounds over the next year. I'm also hopeful that many of these health issues -- diabetes, high blood pressure, and the problems associated with both -- will also go away, giving me a good shot at living to see my 50th birthday.)

February 2009
Okay, the sight of Mr. DeMartino in full American Beauty glory is enough to make you dig your eyes out with a rusty spork, but it was the only Valentine's Day-related alter-ego I had (forgive me, please, I beg you!). A much more pleasant image can be found here, courtesy of S.C. We also have lots of related fanfic on the holiday-themed fanfic page, and of course, you can get your fill of romantic and shippery fanfic goodness here!

Finally, a sad note: Martin Sylvester reports that his site, The Irony Maiden, is no more. Its demise hits the international Daria community hard, as it was the place to go for Daria information outside the USA. Until/unless the Padre can resurrect it (no pun intended), I'll take the opportunity to thank him for providing a great resource for all these many years. It'll be missed!
