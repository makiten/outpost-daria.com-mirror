The Writing Staff

Daria's success is due to many factors, not the least of which is its writing staff. The following people are responsible for the scripts that have both entertained and irritated the fans over the years. Each author is listed alphabetically by their last name; in the case of writing teams, the name of the first credited author is used.

Much of the information listed here comes from various sources, including fan contributions and the Internet Movie Database. In addition, links to their IMDB pages are provided, where you can find more complete and up-to-date information.
